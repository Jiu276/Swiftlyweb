Frequently Asked Questions

How do you choose which products to review?

We select products based on market relevance, user interest, and potential impact. We consider both flagship and budget options, prioritizing products that our readers are most likely to purchase or have questions about.

Do you accept products from manufacturers?

Yes, we accept review units from manufacturers, but this never influences our editorial opinion. We maintain strict editorial independence and will always disclose when a product was provided for review. We also purchase products independently when necessary.

How long does your testing process take?

Our testing process typically takes 2-4 weeks depending on the product category. Laptops and smartphones require extensive battery testing and long-term use evaluation, while accessories might have a shorter testing period.

Can I suggest a product for review?

Absolutely! We welcome product suggestions from our readers. Use the contact form above with "Product Review Request" as the subject, and tell us what you'd like us to review and why.

Do you offer consulting services?

We focus primarily on our editorial content, but we do consider special consulting projects on a case-by-case basis. Please reach out via our partnerships email for more information.

How can I advertise on your website?

We offer various advertising opportunities while maintaining our editorial independence. Contact our partnerships team for information about sponsored content, display advertising, and other promotional opportunities.
